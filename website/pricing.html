Common questions

Is payment automatic or manual?

Manual UPI payment. You pay via UPI, upload the screenshot on the subscribe page, and our team verifies it (usually within a few hours).

Can I track multiple people?

Yes. CAs and advisors can track multiple clients under one account. Each person is priced independently based on their gains.

What happens when my subscription expires?

Your local data is always yours — you can still add, edit, and delete transactions. Live prices and Capital Gains / Tax reports require an active subscription.

Is my financial data sent to the server?

No. All your transaction data stays on your device, encrypted with AES-256. The server only provides live market prices.
